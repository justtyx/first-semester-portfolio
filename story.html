Story without words

Project brief

A story without words was one of two basic content projects. During the period of three days me and my team created a short video telling a story through a language of film rather than sending a message by saying it with words. The video focuses on a girl who is being emotionally affected by the cold season and starts looking for ways to brighten up the soul crushing atmosphere.

Acquired competences

I became more experienced at coming up with quick and creative solutions, learnt more about film language, understood how to translate emotions and tell a story without describing it with words. It helped to build a more quality basis for the next project. Got to plan, shoot and edit a video leading to being more experienced at video editing and getting acquainted with Adobe Premiere Pro.
