Basic content

Theme brief

The objective of the theme was to learn basic content development principles from the beginning to the end. It consisted of two video-related projects, therefore, not only did I learn the process of executing a project but also got acquainted with filming techniques, video language, video pre-production stages and post-production using video editing software (Adobe Premiere Pro).

Acquired competences

Improved my working in a team skills, strategizing project flow, using such programs as Trello for having a more structured workflow of a big project. I learned the video production stages and terminology within the video sphere. Learned the importance of basing decisions on research, neuromarketing strategies on social medias and got acquainted to the basic media laws.
